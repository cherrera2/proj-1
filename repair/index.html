Repair

Chromebooks

Dell: Chromebook 11 3180

Screen Replacement

  1. Remove 4 plastic coverings
  2. Remove 4 screws behind coverings
  3. Remove front plastic boarder
  4. Remove 4 screws holding screen in
  5. Unhinge and remove display cable
  6. Connect display cable to replacement display
  7. Do steps 2-5 in reverse order

Battery Replacement

  1. Remove 2 plastic coverings on bottom
  2. Remove 2 screws behind coverings and 6 others on bottom
  3. Remove bottom
  4. Remove 3 screws holding battery in
  5. Remove battery cable
  6. Connect battery cable to replacement battery
  7. Do steps 2-5 in reverse order

Charger Replacement

  1. Remove 2 plastic coverings on bottom
  2. Remove 2 screws behind coverings and 6 others on bottom
  3. Remove bottom
  4. Remove screw holding charger input in
  5. Remove cover and disconnect charger input
  6. Connect replacement charger input
  7. Do steps 2-5 in reverse order
